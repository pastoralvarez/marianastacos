🌶 Who are we

One family,
one recipe,
one passion.

At Mariana’s Tacos, family and flavor are our priority. We are a family-owned catering company based in Monrovia, California, and we have proudly served our community and neighboring cities for five years.

What began as a simple taco stand has grown into a full-service catering company and a welcoming restaurant.

At Mariana’s Tacos, every event is personalized, and every dish is prepared with care.
